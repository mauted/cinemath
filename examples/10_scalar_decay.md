# Scalar decay (QFT warm-up)

Consider the Lagrangian involving two real scalar fields Φ and φ:

  L = 1/2 (∂_μ Φ)² - 1/2 M² Φ² + 1/2 (∂_μ φ)² - 1/2 m² φ² - μ Φ φ φ

The last term allows a Φ particle to decay into two φ's when M > 2m.
Assuming that condition, compute to lowest order in μ the decay rate Γ of Φ,
and find the lifetime τ = 1/Γ.
